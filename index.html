American Bandogge Size

The American Bandogge is a large and muscular breed. Males typically stand between 24 to 29 inches (61 to 74 cm) tall at the shoulder, while females are slightly smaller, ranging from 22 to 27 inches (56 to 69 cm). They usually weigh between 80 to 140 pounds (36 to 64 kg).

American Bandogge Personality

The American Bandogge is known for its good nature and affectionate disposition, but it can also exhibit high energy and hyperactivity. To address these traits, early socialization is essential to provide them with outlets to burn off energy and prevent reactivity towards people or other dogs. Consistent training from a young age is crucial for their overall well-being and behavior as adults. Positive reinforcement training should be used consistently and firmly, and seeking the assistance of a professional trainer is advisable if behavioral issues arise.

American Bandogge Care


The American Bandogge is known for its good-natured and loving disposition, but it can also exhibit high energy and hyperactivity. Socialization from a young age is crucial to provide opportunities for them to burn off energy and prevent reactivity towards people or other dogs. Consistent training and positive reinforcement from an early age improve their quality of life and address behavioral issues. In terms of exercise, the Bandogge has high requirements and ideally needs a 45-minute to an hour-long daily walk, along with indoor or outdoor play. Due to their size and energy levels, they are not suitable as apartment dogs and may be banned in some areas.

American Bandogge Feeding

To meet the dietary needs of the energetic breed, it is important to provide them diet that meets their nutritional requirements. Opt for reputable dog food brands that offer a complete and balanced diet, with an emphasis on high-quality protein sources, healthy fats, and essential vitamins and minerals. Portion control is crucial to prevent overfeeding and maintain a healthy weight, considering factors such as age, weight, activity level, and overall condition.
